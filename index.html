About Technova

Technova 2K25 is a college event organized by Dr. DY Patil Polytechnic, Kasaba Bawda, Kolhapur. It is a technical fest that showcases students' talents through various technical and innovative competitions. The event provides a platform for students to present posters, demonstrate graphic skills, participate in paper presentations, and compete in multiple events. It offers an opportunity to enhance their skills and win exciting prizes!

Join us for an unforgettable experience filled with innovation, creativity, and celebration. Let's make Technova 2K25 a grand success!
